Our Story

Technically Sound in IT & Engineering Solutions for Africa

In Bovet Engineering, everything we do, we believe in challenging the status quo. We believe in thinking differently. Our primary expertise lies in ICT, Telecommunications, CCTV, and Electrical Engineering — systems that power Nigeria's oil & gas, industrial, and commercial sectors.

We make our services simple to deploy, reliable to operate, and backed by comprehensive after-sales support. From structured cabling and server rooms to telecoms towers and power distribution — we ensure clients get the best outcome while having complete peace of mind.

Founded on the pillars of safety, respect, collaboration, integrity, and innovation, Bovet Engineering has built a reputation for delivering projects on time, within budget, and to clients' expected outcomes across Rivers State and Nigeria.

"We don't just bring Precision in everything we do — we make sure our clients get the desired outcome, giving way for long-time resilience."
